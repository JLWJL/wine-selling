OUR OUTSTANDING WINES

THORN-CLARKE SANDPIPER BAROSSA CABERNET SAUVIGNON

Category:

Another classy wine from Dry River consultant Ant MacKenzie, this is far better than the price. (5 Stars Raymond Chan, 5 Stars Wine Orbit Magazine). It captures that ripe Hawkes Bay fruit - but not in heavy way. Instead it is perfectly focussed and balanced, with defined red and black fruit character set in a quite rich, warming yet fresh and balanced palate with all the finesse of a European wine...or an expensive Kiwi one. Hardly surprising from this consistently superb label.

Elsewhere up to $33.99
